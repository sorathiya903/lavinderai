# 🤖 AI FAQ Chatbot Generator

> Turn your business FAQs into a live chatbot — instantly.

An AI-powered SaaS tool that allows businesses to create and deploy their own FAQ chatbot in seconds. Simply enter your business details, FAQs, and get a fully functional chatbot with a unique endpoint.

👉 https://messagegpt.indevs.in

---

## ✨ Features

- 🧠 AI-powered FAQ responses  
- 🔗 Unique endpoint for every business (`/your-business-name`)  
- ⚡ Instant chatbot generation  
- 🗂️ Stores data using Firebase  
- 🎨 Customizable chatbot UI (name, content, etc.)  
- 📱 Lightweight and fast  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript  
- **Backend:** Flask (Python)  
- **Database:** Firebase Realtime Database  

---

## 📦 How It Works

1. Enter your business name  
2. Add FAQ / content  
3. Click **Generate Chatbot**  
4. Get a unique link like: https://yourdomain.com/my-business
5. Share it with your customers 🚀
